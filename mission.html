MISSION

We consider the protection of the environment as our primary responsibility. In this regard, all our processes and technologies are implemented to work in that direction. A clean and a green environment is an absolute necessity and we ensure this by using state-of-the-art Effluent Treatment Plant for all the waste generated. Our sprawling green campus houses a large percentage of our employees. We actively support and fund various environmental organizations in their crusade to make this place a better world to live in.
